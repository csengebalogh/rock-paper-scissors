How To Play

'Rock, Paper, Scissors' is a simple hand game with many names and variations.

The rules require that competing players use one hand to form one of three shapes at an agreed-upon time. The person that plays the strongest “object” is the winner of the game. It's that easy!

In this case, you're about to play this game against a computer. Once you pick your shape, the computer does so, and the winner is announced immediately.

The three rules are as follows:

  • Rock beats scissors.
  • Scissors beat paper.
  • Paper beats rock.
